About Cuberootinc.

Cuberootinc. is a student-led tech club at Delhi Public School, Mysore. We focus on coding, collaboration, and real-world project building — run entirely by students.

This hackathon is our first major school-level initiative. We’re designing it to be hands-on, competitive, and fun — open to students across grades. We believe tech is best learned by building.

We also treat this like a real company — branding, outreach, website, and communication are all student-handled under the Cuberootinc. name.
